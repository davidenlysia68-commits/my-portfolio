My Process: From Idea to Results

How I Work

My Process: From Strategy to Results

📱 Social Media Management

01
🔍
Brand Discovery & Audit
I learn everything about your business — goals, audience, and competitors. I audit your existing pages, engagement rates, and content to build the right foundation.
02
📋
Strategy & Content Planning
I build a custom strategy defining your brand voice, content pillars, posting frequency, and platform priorities — with a full content calendar planned two weeks ahead.
03
🎨
Content Creation & Design
I create all visuals using Canva and Photoshop — graphics, carousels, and story templates — paired with engaging, on-brand captions optimized for each platform.
04
📅
Scheduling & Publishing
All content is scheduled in advance using Zoho Social for consistent posting. I leave space for reactive content — trending topics and real-time moments relevant to your brand.
05
💬
Community Management
Every day I respond to comments, DMs, and mentions — promptly and in your brand voice. I proactively engage with your target audience to build a genuine community.
06
📊
Analytics & Optimization
Weekly metric reviews and monthly performance reports — reach, engagement, follower growth, and top content. Every decision is data-driven, never guesswork.

🎯 Digital Marketing & Paid Advertising

01
🎯
Goal Setting & Market Research
I define clear, measurable goals with you — leads, traffic, sales, or awareness. Then I research your target market in depth and analyze competitor ad strategies for opportunities.
02
👥
Audience Building & Targeting
I build precise custom and lookalike audiences segmented by demographics, interests, behavior, and purchase intent — ensuring every ad reaches the right person at the right time.
03
✍️
Ad Creative & Copywriting
Compelling graphics, short videos, and carousels paired with persuasive copy — every ad has a clear, strong call to action designed to drive your specific desired result.
04
🚀
Campaign Setup & Launch
Full campaign structure setup with tracking pixels, conversion events, UTM parameters, and A/B testing from day one — identifying top-performing combinations before scaling.
05
⚙️
Active Monitoring & Optimization
Daily campaign monitoring — adjusting bids, pausing underperformers, scaling winners, refreshing creatives. Active management is what separates good results from great ones.
06
📈
Reporting & Scaling
Weekly snapshots and monthly reports explaining what the numbers mean for your business — with a clear improvement plan and scaling strategy to maximize your ROI.
